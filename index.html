Controls

You move by using the arrow keys or keys below:

          u i o    7 8 9
           \|/      \|/
          j- -k    4- -6
           /|\      /|\
          n m ,    1 2 3

Note: in some levels you must be able to move diagonally!

During the game, there are two action keys that use (and consume) your possessions:
  • w or 5: Whip
  • T: Teleport
